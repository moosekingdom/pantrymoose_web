How the day flows

One system for the whole day.

Pantry Moose keeps the front desk, volunteer desk, donation entry, and reporting connected so your team does not have to bounce between paper, spreadsheets, and separate tools.

1

Volunteers check in fast

Use a simple kiosk screen to sign volunteers in and out and keep a clear view of who is helping today.

2

Clients get checked in quickly

Record household counts, notes, and visits without slowing down the front desk when the line is moving.

3

Food weight gets recorded later

Check clients in first, then record distribution weight after the visit is complete so the front desk can stay focused.

4

Leaders review reports and trends

Run reports on visits, households, pounds distributed, donations, and volunteer activity from the same system your team uses every day.
